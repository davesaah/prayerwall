# PrayerWall

A web application for prayer requests and wall of prayer.

## Features

### Authentication

- Login/Register with Google.
- Login/Register with Email and Password.

### Adding Prayer Requests

- Add a prayer request using a rich text editor.

### Viewing Prayer Requests (Intercession & Encouragement)

- All registered users can view all prayer requests.
- Everyone can send words of encouragement after interceding for the prayer request.
- The Bible search button can be used to find and insert verses while giving encouragement.

### Viewing Prayer Requests (Testimonies)

- Only the author of a prayer request can mark the prayer as answered and share a testimony.
- Answered prayers remain on the prayer wall to serve as a testimony to the faithfulness of God and to encourage others.

### Privacy Terms

- Everyone is anonymous on the platform.
- Kindly eliminate sharing of Personal Identifiable Information (PII) when interacting on the platform to stay safe online.

### Terms of Use

- Treat everything with gentleness and love.
- No prayer request is too small or too big to be shared.
- Also pray for what you share; don't just drop and run. God is not isolated to a few 'prayer warriors'; He wants to have a personal relationship with you.
